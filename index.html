Introducing The Canvas Project for Hangouts Meet

The Canvas Project

Great ideas happen when we come together

We worked with artists to create unique works of art that visualize collaboration and the
great ideas that happen when we build something together. We’re calling it The Canvas
Project.
And should your collaborators be halfway around the world, you can now pick one of
these artworks to set the backdrop for your next get-together over Hangouts Meet.

Meet the artists

Influenced by the power of collaboration and connection these artists created both
photographic and illustrative wallpapers for the Meet experience.

We’ll be collaborating with more artists,
so check back for new artworks.
